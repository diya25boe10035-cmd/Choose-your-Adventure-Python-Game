Problem statement: Bring Your Own Project (BYOP)

As part of Introduction to Problem Solving and Programming course, I have completed my Bring Your Own Project — a capstone activity designed to push me beyond exercises and into real problem-solving.

Scope of the project: The main scope of my project is in entertainment. It is a great way to relieve boredom while still employing the frontal lobe on easy mode.
Imagination is paramount here. This project would be greatly appreciated by avid readers. Showcasing a creative twist on the classical linear story-telling.

Target users: Children, adolescents or any individual seeking to better their decision-making skills or simply looking for a fun way to pass the time.
