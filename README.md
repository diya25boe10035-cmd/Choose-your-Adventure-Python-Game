# Choose-your-Adventure-Python-Game
An interactive story game in python where the player gets to decide how the story unfolds by choosing from multiple pre-determined options. 
The story has three separate endings, where the reader assumes the protagonist's role and thereby makes choices determining between the good, the bad and the third ending. The bad ending is obviously death. It is upto interpretation whether the third ending is good, bad, neither or both. The reader gets to decide what they consider a good ending.

The keywords for each choice is put in square brackets []. It is only by inputting the word in the brackets that the player can move on to the next sequence in the story. Any other input will show game over or take the player back to the beginning, essentially resetting the game.

One of the choices incorporates a Mad-Libs mini game, where the player is asked to choose the following:

Miraculous cure: Basically asking the player to name the object they hope to obtain at the end of their quest. Can be a flower or an artifact or even just a nonsensical word.

Reason: The reason the player decided to venture out on this quest or what they are going to use the object they attain for.

Name: Any name is fine 

Place: Any place is fine. Can be real or from the imagination.

Adjective: Any adjective is fine. Although it is suggested that the player choose something grand like Destruction or Miracles as the preceeding words are "Lord of..".

Kingdom: Name a kingdom. Can be a real name or something entirely from your imagination.

These are only suggestions as the essence of Mad-libs is to have fun, irregardless of narrative or common sense.

The good ending hides one last Mad-lib where the player. Finally the player is asked if they would like to play again, yes or no. Choosing yes will start the story from the beginning all over again. Any other answer will end the game.
